Renovation Cost Data

Know What You Are Getting Into

One of the most unique features is our Property Renovation Calculator. This tool was designed by real estate investors, for real estate investors with one goal in mind: to help you make more informed decisions so you can reach new levels of success. Whether you are buying a complete remodel or just need to put new floors and paint in the house, the Property Renovation Calculator can help you avoid mistakes and estimate your returns through detailed but easy-to-use analysis. This tool will revolutionize the way you think about buying a used property and help you efficiently and effectively predict your financial position with the property.

Hyperlocal Data

We Have a "Locals Only" Policy

You don’t look at the entire country when starting your investment search, so why should we? We target the markets that we know and love. Doing this allows an increase in data integrity over our competitors. We have realtors in each of our markets spot checking our predictions on a daily basis. While those other guys are trying to tackle every market at a respectable level, we have decided to PERFECT a smaller number of markets to provide you with the best experience possible.
